The Mandate

About AIT MUN

As shadows lengthen across the corridors of power and the whispers of the world grow louder, the free peoples must convene. AIT Model United Nations 2026 is not merely a simulation — it is a summons to the highest levels of statesmanship.

Delegates will navigate complex geopolitical landscapes, balancing ancestral claims, strategic alliances, and the looming challenges that define our age. Within these councils, every speech carries weight, every alliance builds strength.

Step into the chamber with conviction. History is written by those who dare to rise when the world needs leaders the most.

Deliberations

The High Councils

Continuous Crisis Committee (CCC)

A fast-paced committee where the situation evolves every moment. Delegates must react to classified crises, form quick alliances, and take strategic decisions that can change the course of events—much like the unexpected trials faced across Middle-earth.

Agenda 2026

United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

The council responsible for maintaining global peace and security. Delegates will address the escalating Middle East tensions involving the US, Israel, and Iran, debating proxy warfare and retaliatory strikes while striving to prevent further conflict.

Agenda 2026

United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

A forum dedicated to protecting fundamental human rights. Delegates will examine how emerging military technologies and artificial intelligence impact privacy, freedom, and dignity in an increasingly digital world.

Agenda 2026

United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime (UNODC)

This committee tackles organized crime, narcotics trafficking, and international criminal networks. Delegates will discuss the impact of external interference on crime networks, with a special focus on regions such as Venezuela and Iran.

Agenda 2026

All India Political Parties Meet (AIPPM)

A dynamic national committee where Indian political leaders debate pressing domestic issues. Delegates will deliberate on the regulation of social media platforms, addressing misinformation, surveillance concerns, and constitutional free speech safeguards.

Agenda 2026

International Press (IP)

The storytellers of the conference. Journalists and photographers will document debates, expose controversies, and shape narratives, ensuring transparency while capturing every defining moment of the council.
